About Us

At Shegar Coffee, we believe coffee is more than a drink — it’s a tradition that brings people together. Our story begins in the heart of Ethiopia, where coffee was first discovered and where every cup still carries the warmth of connection and culture. Inspired by our Oromo roots and the spirit of buna, we created Shegar Coffee as a space for comfort, community, and conversation. Each bean we brew is carefully selected and roasted with love, honoring the farmers, the land, and the generations who have kept this tradition alive. Whether you’re sharing stories with friends, working over your favorite cup, or simply taking a quiet moment for yourself — Shegar Coffee is your home away from home. Because here, every sip tells a story, and every story starts with a good cup of coffee.
